# Green Desert Life - Project Notes

## Project Overview

Green Desert Life is a nature-oriented brand based in Malatya, Türkiye.

The project combines local agricultural production, sustainable living, natural products and ethical trade principles.

The website should feel like a living ecosystem rather than a conventional e-commerce store.

---

## Core Products

* Apricot
* Mountain Tea
* Lavender
* Mountain Thyme

Future products should remain consistent with the brand's natural and sustainable identity.

---

## Design Philosophy

The current design language should be preserved.

Do not redesign the project unnecessarily.

Improvements should enhance the existing experience rather than replace it.

The website should communicate:

* Nature
* Authenticity
* Trust
* Simplicity
* Sustainability
* Craftsmanship

---

## User Experience Goals

Visitors should feel:

* Connected to nature
* Connected to producers
* Safe while shopping
* Inspired by the philosophy of the brand

The website should not feel like a generic marketplace.

## Logo Concept

The logo represents:

* The letter G
* Leaf symbolism
* Sand dunes
* Water drop
* Life emerging from difficult conditions

The logo must remain organic and natural.

Avoid futuristic or corporate-looking redesigns.

---

## Typography

Preferred:

* Clean
* Readable
* Elegant
* Warm

Avoid:

* Excessive decoration
* Aggressive commercial styles
* Overly technical appearance

---

## Content Strategy

Content should emphasize:

* Nature
* Sustainability
* Traditional knowledge
* Local production
* Honest trade
* Human connection

Avoid aggressive sales language.

Storytelling is preferred over direct advertising.

---

## SEO

Every page should include:

* Descriptive titles
* Meta descriptions
* Alt text for images
* Semantic HTML
* Structured content hierarchy

---

## Performance

Prioritize:

* Fast loading
* Mobile-first design
* Optimized images
* Accessibility
* Clean code

Avoid unnecessary dependencies.

---

## Development Rules

Before making changes:

1. Analyze existing structure.
2. Preserve current design philosophy.
3. Do not remove working functionality.
4. Do not introduce unnecessary frameworks.
5. Keep Vercel deployment compatibility.
6. Optimize before replacing.
7. Maintain responsive behavior.

Always read MARKA_MANIFESTOSU.md before implementing major design or content changes.
